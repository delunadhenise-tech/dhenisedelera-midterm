Choosing Based on Size

Remember that "bread" size matters! A Great Dane needs a lot of couch space, while a Yorkie can fit in a small carry-on bag.

  • Small: 2-20 lbs (Great for apartments)
  • Medium: 21-50 lbs (Versatile companions)
  • Large: 50-90+ lbs (Need plenty of exercise)

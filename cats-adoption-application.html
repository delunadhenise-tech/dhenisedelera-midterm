The Feline Adoption Guide

Adopting a cat is a 15 to 20-year commitment to a new family member. At PurrPets, we want to ensure your home is ready for a "purr-fect" match.

"A house is not a home without the pitter-patter of kitty paws."

The Adoption Checklist

Before you fill out the application below, check if you have these three essentials ready:

  • The Safe Room: A quiet space where the cat can decompress for the first 48 hours.
  • The Vertical Empire: At least one cat tree or high shelf for safety.
  • The Vet Plan: A local clinic identified for your "Gotcha Day" wellness exam.

The "Slow Intro" Method

If you have other pets, never just "drop" the new cat in the middle of the room. Use Scent Swapping (rubbing a cloth on one pet and letting the other smell it) for at least a week before they meet face-to-face!

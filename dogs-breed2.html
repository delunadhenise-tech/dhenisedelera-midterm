The Dog Breeds

From the fastest runners to the fluffiest lap dogs, PurrPets helps you identify the perfect "bread" for your home.

Breed Gallery

Breed Name Photo Traits/Specialty
Bulldog Distinctive "sourmug" face, muscular build, and gentle, affectionate nature, making them excellent family companions.
Alaskan malamute A loyal and bold companion.
Airedale terrier They are loyal, friendly, curious, energetic dogs who are fun loving, eager and tireless.
Pug They are best known for their wrinkly, flat faces (brachycephalic), large, dark, expressive eyes, and curly tails.
French Bulldog Playful, adaptable, and great for city living.
Rottweiler Known for its powerful build, extreme loyalty, and steady, confident temperament.
Affenpinscher They are confident, fearless, and often comical companions that were originally bred to hunt rats in kitchens and stables.
Australian Cattle Dog Extreme intelligence, loyalty, and boundless energy, these dogs are highly trainable.
Border Collie Highly intelligent, energetic workaholics originally bred for herding sheep on the British borderlands.
American Staffordshire Terrier A muscular, intelligent, and confident breed known for its deep loyalty to family and "velcro" personality.
Australian Shepherd Extreme intelligence and versatility, they are characterized by an intense work drive, a strong desire to please, and striking, varied coat colors.
Bichon Frisé small, sturdy, and hypoallergenic dog breed renowned for its cheerful, affectionate, and charming personality.

Which Breed Fits You?

If you want a dog that runs with you, a Border Collie is best. If you want a dog that naps with you, a Frenchie or Shih Tzu is the way to go!

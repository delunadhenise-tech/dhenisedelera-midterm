Feline Health & Wellness

A healthy cat is a happy cat! At PurrPets, we believe that regular check-ups and a stress-free environment are the keys to a long, 20-year life for your feline companion.


Cats are experts at hiding illness; watch for subtle changes in behavior.

Common Health Milestones

Keep track of your cat's medical needs with this basic schedule:

Life Stage Essential Care Frequency
Kitten FVRCP Vaccines & Deworming Every 3-4 weeks until 16 weeks.
Adult Dental Cleaning & Rabies Shot Annual Exam.
Senior (10+) Blood Work & Kidney Screening Every 6 months.

Signs Your Cat Needs a Vet

If you notice any of these "Red Flags," contact your veterinarian immediately:

  • Hiding: If your social cat is suddenly hiding under the bed.
  • Litter Box Changes: Straining or going outside the box.
  • Weight Loss: Sudden changes in appetite or body condition.
  • Bad Breath: Can indicate dental disease or kidney issues.

The "Purr" Myth

Did you know cats don't just purr when they are happy? They also purr to self-heal! The frequency of a cat's purr (25-150 Hertz) can actually help improve bone density and heal tendons.

Fast Feline Facts

  • Sleepyheads: Cats spend an average of 12 to 16 hours a day sleeping.
  • Unique Prints: A cat's nose print is unique, much like a human fingerprint.
  • Communication: Cats rarely "meow" at other cats; they save that sound specifically to talk to humans!

Pro-Tip: Enrichment

An indoor cat needs mental stimulation! Ensure you have vertical space (like cat trees) and interactive toys to keep their hunting instincts sharp.

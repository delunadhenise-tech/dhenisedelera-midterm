Hamster Adoption Center

Small pets have big needs! At PurrPets, we want to ensure every hamster finds a home with plenty of space to burrow and a wheel that won't hurt their back.

The "Gotcha Day" Checklist 🐹

  • Large Bin or Tank (450+ sq in)
  • 6+ Inches of Paper Bedding
  • Appropriate Sized Silent Wheel
  • Sand Bath (Chinchilla Sand)
  • High-Protein Seed Mix
  • Multiple Hidey Houses

The Rabbit Adoption Path

Are you ready to be a "Bunny Parent"? At PurrPets, we ensure our rabbits go to homes where they are treated as indoor family members, not backyard accessories.

Pre-Adoption Checklist

  • Exotic Vet: Have you found a vet that treats rabbits? (Standard dog/cat vets often won't).
  • Bunny Proofing: Are your "spicy noodles" (wires) covered?
  • Hay Storage: Do you have a cool, dry place for a large bale of Timothy hay?

The "Social Potato" Commitment 🥬

Because Guinea Pigs are herd animals, PurrPets primarily adopts out bonded pairs. If you are adopting a single piggie, you must already have a compatible friend waiting for them at home!

Guinea Pig Adoption Application

Please provide the following details to help us find the perfect herd for your home:

Requirement Checklist Applicant Confirmation
Cage Dimensions: (e.g., 2x4 C&C Grid or larger)

Vitamin C Plan: Daily fresh bell peppers/veggies provided.
Bedding Type: Fleece liners / Aspen / Paper-based.
Floor Time Space: Safe, flat area for daily exercise.

Adoption Note: Guinea Pigs can live 5–8 years. Please ensure you are ready for a long-term commitment to these chatty little friends!

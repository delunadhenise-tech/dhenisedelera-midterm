The "Solitary" Rule

At PurrPets, we strictly follow the One Hamster, One Cage rule for Syrian hamsters. They are extremely territorial and will fight if forced to share a space. Dwarf hamsters can sometimes live in pairs, but even then, they often prefer their own bachelor pad!

Night Owl Warning: Hamsters are crepuscular/nocturnal. This means they sleep while you're at school or work and wake up just as you're heading to bed. Make sure their wheel is "silent" or you might hear a tiny marathon at 3:00 AM!
